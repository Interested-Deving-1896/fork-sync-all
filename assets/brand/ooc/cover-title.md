## Sync · Mirror · Automate

**fork-sync-all** is the control plane for the `OpenOS-Project-Ecosystem-OOC`
GitHub org and its GitLab mirror at `openos-project-ooc-ecosystem`. It mirrors
repositories across the org chain, manages READMEs, syncs upstream forks, and
runs org-wide maintenance workflows.
